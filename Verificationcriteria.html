Detailed Verification Criteria

Our comprehensive verification process ensures the highest standards of trust and credibility on the Oriontec platform.

1 Identity Verification Requirements

  • Government-issued photo identification (passport, driver's license, or national ID)
  • Clear, high-resolution scans or photos of the front and back of your ID
  • Proof of address (utility bill, bank statement, or official correspondence dated within the last 3 months)
  • A selfie photo holding your ID and a handwritten note with "Oriontec" and the current date
  • Phone number verification through SMS confirmation
  • Email address verification through confirmation link
  • Facial recognition check to match your selfie with your ID photo
  • All documents must be valid and not expired

Your personal information is protected with enterprise-grade encryption and is only used for verification purposes. We comply with all relevant data protection regulations.

2 Professional Credentials

  • Business registration documents (if applying as a business entity)
  • Professional certifications relevant to your industry
  • Portfolio of work or case studies demonstrating expertise
  • LinkedIn profile or professional website showing relevant experience
  • References from clients or professional associates (minimum of 2)
  • Proof of professional membership in relevant industry associations
  • Educational credentials and qualifications
  • Published works, patents, or other intellectual property (if applicable)

Different industries may have specific credential requirements. Our verification team will guide you on the most relevant documentation for your field.

3 Security Compliance

  • Completion of Oriontec's security assessment questionnaire
  • Implementation of two-factor authentication on your account
  • Secure password meeting our complexity requirements
  • Clean security record with no history of data breaches or security incidents
  • Compliance with industry-specific security standards (if applicable)
  • Regular security updates and patch management for connected systems
  • Adherence to our data handling and privacy guidelines
  • Successful completion of basic security awareness training

Security requirements are designed to protect both your account and our community. Our team can provide guidance on meeting these standards.

4 Community Standing

  • Minimum account age of 3 months
  • No history of policy violations or community guidelines infractions
  • Positive feedback rating of at least 4.5/5 from other users
  • No unresolved disputes or complaints from other community members
  • Demonstrated adherence to Oriontec's code of conduct
  • Positive engagement with the community through comments, forums, or support
  • No evidence of spamming, harassment, or other prohibited behaviors
  • Consistent professional conduct in all platform interactions

New accounts may be granted exceptions to the 3-month requirement if they can provide substantial external credentials and references.

5 Activity Requirements

  • Regular login activity (minimum once per week over the past 3 months)
  • Completion of at least 5 transactions or projects on the platform
  • Active participation in community discussions or forums
  • Consistent quality of work or services provided through the platform
  • Timely responses to messages and inquiries (average response time under 24 hours)
  • Regular profile updates and maintenance
  • Contribution of valuable content or resources to the community
  • Engagement with platform features and tools

Activity requirements demonstrate your commitment to the platform and help us ensure that verified accounts are actively contributing to our community.

6 Review Process

  • Complete application submission with all required documentation
  • Initial screening within 48 hours to confirm application completeness
  • Thorough review by our verification team (5-7 business days)
  • Possible request for additional information or clarification
  • Final decision communicated via email with detailed feedback
  • If approved, verification badge activation within 24 hours
  • If rejected, detailed explanation and guidance for reapplication
  • Annual review process to maintain verification status

Our review process is thorough to maintain the integrity of the verification program. You can check your application status at any time in your account dashboard.
